Fiscal Resilience Explained

Fiscal resilience is the capacity of a government to effectively manage its fiscal resources in the face of economic shocks, disasters, and other unforeseen events. It involves maintaining fiscal stability and sustainability while being able to respond to and recover from adverse situations.
